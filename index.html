  • Beats per minute can be set in one of three ways:
    1. Entering a number directly into the top input box
    2. Pressing the '+' and '-' buttons either side of the input box
    3. Tapping the 'Tap Tempo' button two or more times
  • The time signature defaults to 4/4, but can be changed to any simple (4) or compound (8) time required.
  • Beats per minute are measure either in crotchet beats (simple time) or dotted crotchet beats (compound time).
